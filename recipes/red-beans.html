Red Beans and Rice

Description

This is easily my best go to recipe when in a pinch with company coming over later in the day. It is simple and everyone loves it, especially when they think you worked all day on it and it only took just a few minutes to throw together!

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Andouille Sausage
  • Beef Broth
  • Red Beans
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot. Add oinion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add bell pepper and garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add sausage and cook until they release oil.
  4. Add beans and broth at the same time. Add water to be sure all ingredients are covered in liquid.
  5. Add seasoning to your linking. Tony's work great!
  6. Be sure to stir to prevent sticking and cook down until beeans are soft and flavorful. Add water to desired thinkness.
